The system in three pillars

Follicle Intelligence operates through a clear, repeatable structure: analysis, scoring, and improvement. Each pillar feeds the next.

01

Analyse

Structured case review against defined criteria. Extraction patterns, density, placement, and consistency are examined so variation is visible, not assumed.

02

Score

Benchmarked scoring that places performance against standards and against prior cases. You see where you stand — objectively.

03

Improve

Actionable feedback that directs the next case. Improvement is targeted, not diffuse. Each cycle builds on the last.

HairAudit: the clinical application

HairAudit is the clinical application of Follicle Intelligence — the platform where analysis, scoring, and feedback become practice.

Through HairAudit, cases are uploaded, audited against benchmarks, and returned with structured feedback. It handles scoring and comparison so that progression is visible to the surgeon and to the institute. No other training pathway offers this level of integrated, audit-backed development. It is unique globally.

Outcomes are not assumed; they are measured, benchmarked, and fed back into the next case.

Closed-loop training model

Follicle Intelligence turns development into a system, not a concept.

  1. Trainee performs the case
  2. Upload to the audit system
  3. Audit — analysis and scoring
  4. Feedback — structured, actionable
  5. Improve — next case informed by data

The loop repeats. Progression is continuous and measurable.
