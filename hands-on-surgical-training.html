Surgical Training Pathway

A five-stage progression from scientific foundations to independent surgical practice with ongoing mentorship and performance monitoring.

1

Scientific Foundations

Comprehensive trichology and hair loss diagnosis training. Understanding patient assessment, pattern recognition, and treatment planning before any surgical intervention.

  • Hair loss classification and diagnosis
  • Patient consultation frameworks
  • Treatment planning protocols
2

Extraction Mastery

Precise control of follicular unit extraction techniques under direct supervision. Focus on minimizing trauma and maximizing graft viability.

  • Punch control and biomechanics
  • Depth control techniques
  • Transection avoidance strategies
  • Graft handling and preservation
3

Implantation Mastery

Advanced implantation techniques emphasizing natural results, proper angulation, and density optimization.

  • Angle design and direction
  • Density planning algorithms
  • Hairline artistry and design
  • Recipient site creation
4

Independent Surgery

Full case planning and execution with oversight. Integration of extraction and implantation skills into complete surgical procedures.

  • Complete case planning
  • Full surgical execution
  • Decision-making under supervision
  • Complication management
5

Continuous Performance Monitoring

AI-powered surgical auditing through Follicle Intelligence for ongoing quality assessment and continuous improvement.

  • Graft survival analysis
  • Extraction trauma scoring
  • Implantation quality metrics
  • Performance benchmarking

Surgical Mentorship Model

Every stage of training includes direct mentorship from experienced surgeons, ensuring proper technique development and clinical decision-making.

Direct Supervision

One-on-one guidance during all surgical procedures, with immediate feedback and correction.

Case Review Sessions

Regular review of surgical cases with detailed analysis of techniques and outcomes.

Performance Coaching

Individualized coaching based on your specific strengths and areas for improvement.

Long-term Support

Ongoing mentorship even after formal training completion through our alumni program.
