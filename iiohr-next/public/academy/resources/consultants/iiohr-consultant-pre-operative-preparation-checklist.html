Pre-operative coordination checklist

Volume 3 Week 10 · Operational readiness · Not medical clearance — surgeon / anaesthesia / governance as per policy.

Checklist completion supports safe workflow; it does not replace clinician sign-off. If any item creates doubt, document and escalate before proceeding.
Domain☐Notes / escalation
Contact details, arrival time, and escort rules confirmed
Medications & supplements disclosed; changes since last clinical review captured
Allergies and relevant medical updates documented
Fasting or meal rules (if applicable) explained in plain language
Hair / skin preparation per clinic instruction
What to bring (ID, payment process, garments) clarified
Patient verbalises understanding or gaps are flagged
Late concern or new symptom since planning — routed to clinical team

Anxiety vs clinical uncertainty: Routine nerves → calm repetition of process. New symptom, misunderstanding of risk, or mismatch with instructions → pause and escalate.
