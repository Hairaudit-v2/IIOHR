For consultant / coordinator use · Tick to document review · Escalation per clinic protocol — not a substitute for clinical triage.

Presence of a flag does not diagnose a condition; it signals that non-doctor staff should avoid closing the pathway and should complete documentation and clinical routing as directed by policy.
