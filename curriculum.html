Training Curriculum

Comprehensive Surgical Training Program

Eight integrated modules covering the complete spectrum of hair restoration surgery, from fundamental science to advanced surgical techniques and outcome auditing.

Program Structure

The curriculum is designed as a progressive educational pathway, building from foundational science through advanced surgical techniques to outcome auditing and continuous improvement.

Foundation Modules 1–2
Clinical Practice Modules 3–4
Surgical Mastery Modules 5–6
Advanced Practice Modules 7–8

Curriculum Modules

Each module includes theoretical foundations, practical applications, clinical case studies, and references to current literature.

Module 1

Hair Science Foundations

Expand

Fundamental biology of the hair follicle including anatomy, embryology and cellular signaling pathways.

Core Topics

  • Hair follicle embryology
  • Follicular unit anatomy
  • Dermal papilla biology
  • Hair cycle regulation
  • Hormonal drivers of hair loss
  • Nutritional and metabolic influences

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the molecular basis of hair follicle development
  • Identify key cellular components and their functions
  • Analyze hormonal regulation of hair growth cycles

Resources

  • Interactive follicle anatomy diagrams
  • Histological slide library
  • Video: Hair follicle development animation
  • Case study: Hormonal hair loss patterns
Module 2

Classification of Hair Loss

Expand

Comprehensive classification and differential diagnosis of hair loss conditions.

Core Topics

  • Androgenetic alopecia
  • Norwood–Hamilton classification
  • Ludwig classification
  • Telogen effluvium
  • Alopecia areata
  • Scarring alopecias
  • Differential diagnosis framework

Clinical Application

Pattern recognition training with before/after case studies and classification exercises.

Module 3

Clinical Diagnosis of Hair Loss

Expand

Advanced diagnostic techniques and clinical evaluation protocols.

Core Topics

  • Trichoscopy
  • Laboratory investigation protocols
  • Hair density measurement
  • Photographic analysis
  • Clinical history taking

Practical Skills

  • Trichoscope operation and interpretation
  • Diagnostic imaging techniques
  • Patient consultation frameworks
Module 4

Medical Management of Hair Loss

Expand

Evidence-based medical treatments and therapeutic interventions.

Core Topics

  • Minoxidil therapy
  • Finasteride and DHT blockers
  • Hormonal treatments
  • PRP and regenerative therapies
  • Lifestyle and nutritional correction

Evidence Base

Review of clinical trials, systematic reviews, and treatment algorithms with risk-benefit analysis.

Module 5

Hair Transplant Surgery

Expand

Core surgical techniques and procedural fundamentals.

Core Topics

  • Follicular unit extraction
  • Punch selection and skin biomechanics
  • Graft handling
  • Hairline design
  • Density planning
  • Recipient site creation

Surgical Skills

  • Instrumentation and equipment mastery
  • Surgical ergonomics and technique
  • Anatomical landmark identification
Module 6

Advanced Surgical Techniques

Expand

Specialized techniques for complex cases and diverse patient populations.

Core Topics

  • Long hair FUE
  • Afro hair extraction
  • Repair surgery
  • Scar correction
  • Female hairline design

Advanced Applications

Complex case studies and technical refinements for challenging clinical scenarios.

Module 7

Post Operative Management

Expand

Comprehensive post-operative care and outcome optimization.

Core Topics

  • Donor healing

  • PRP and exosomes

  • Complication management

Patient Care Protocols

Surgical Outcome Auditing

Expand

Data-driven quality assessment and continuous performance improvement.

Audit Framework

Comprehensive outcome measurement protocols and quality improvement methodologies.
