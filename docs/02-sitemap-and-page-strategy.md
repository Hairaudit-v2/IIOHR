## Page-by-Page Strategy

### Home
**Purpose:** Establish what IIOHR is, why it’s different, and who it’s for in one scroll.  
**Key messages:** Beyond the short course; from technician to surgeon; training, auditing, mentoring, support.  
**Content roles:** Hero, differentiators, pathway summary, Follicle Intelligence mention, audience fit, faculty tease, primary CTA (Apply/Enquire).  
**CTA:** Apply / Enquire; secondary: Training Pathways, About.

---

### About IIOHR
**Purpose:** Identity, history (Australian Institute → International Institute), mission, and values.  
**Key messages:** Premium training and standards body; advancing surgeons beyond basic short-course education; global reach, Australian discipline.  
**Content roles:** Origin story, mission, what we stand for, why “institute” not “academy,” link to Standards and Training Pathways.  
**CTA:** Training Pathways, Apply / Enquire.

---

### Training Pathways
**Purpose:** Hub for how training is structured — the overall journey, not just one course.  
**Key messages:** Pathway from beginner/intermediate to higher competence; multiple components (practical, theory, auditing, mentoring).  
**Content roles:** Pathway overview (visual or stepped), links to: Practical FUE, Hair Loss Science, Follicle Intelligence, Surgeon Development; who each stage is for.  
**CTA:** Deep-dive into each pathway component; Apply / Enquire.

---

### Practical FUE Training
**Purpose:** Detail hands-on, live-patient FUE training — not simulation-only.  
**Key messages:** Live patient training; real surgery under supervision; skill progression; safety and standards.  
**Content roles:** What’s covered, format (e.g. intensity, duration), live-patient focus, how it fits into the pathway, faculty involvement.  
**CTA:** Surgeon Development Pathway, Apply / Enquire.

---

### Hair Loss Science and Trichology
**Purpose:** Position IIOHR as serious on theory — biology, trichology, diagnosis, planning.  
**Key messages:** Advanced theory; hair loss biology; trichology education; science-informed practice.  
**Content roles:** Curriculum themes, why theory matters for surgeons, integration with practical training, who it’s for (e.g. doctors wanting deeper understanding).  
**CTA:** Training Pathways, Apply / Enquire.

---

### Follicle Intelligence Integration
**Purpose:** Explain how Follicle Intelligence supports post-training development and auditing.  
**Key messages:** Longitudinal auditing; measurable improvement; data-led feedback; post-training use.  
**Content roles:** What Follicle Intelligence is (software/audit tool), how it’s used in training and after, how auditing accelerates improvement, no overclaim — tool in a broader pathway.  
**CTA:** Surgeon Development Pathway, Outcomes, Apply / Enquire.

---

### Surgeon Development Pathway
**Purpose:** Show the full journey — training plus ongoing support, mentoring, auditing.  
**Key messages:** From course completion to continued development; mentoring; audit-led improvement; pathway to excellence (not guaranteed “master” claim).  
**Content roles:** Timeline or stages: training → mentoring → auditing → ongoing support; role of Follicle Intelligence; what “pathway” means.  
**CTA:** Follicle Intelligence, Practical FUE, Apply / Enquire.

---

### Standards and Australian Framework
**Purpose:** Frame Australian surgery standards as the guiding framework for curriculum and discipline.  
**Key messages:** Australian standards inform the program; discipline and rigour; global relevance; not a legal/regulatory overclaim.  
**Content roles:** What we mean by “Australian framework,” how it shapes content and expectations, relevance for international surgeons.  
**CTA:** Training Pathways, About, Apply / Enquire.

---

### For Clinics
**Purpose:** Speak to clinics investing in surgeon development.  
**Key messages:** Structured surgeon development; measurable, standards-aligned training; benefit to the clinic and patients.  
**Content roles:** Why clinics choose IIOHR, what’s included (pathway, auditing, mentoring), options or formats if relevant (e.g. multiple surgeons).  
**CTA:** Training Pathways, Apply / Enquire.

---

### Faculty / Mentors
**Purpose:** Build trust through people — who delivers and supports the pathway.  
**Key messages:** Experienced faculty; mentoring and support; credibility without celebrity hype.  
**Content roles:** Faculty overview (bios, roles, areas of focus), mentor role in development pathway, tone: credible and concise.  
**CTA:** Training Pathways, Apply / Enquire.

---

### Outcomes / Why This Works
**Purpose:** Explain the logic and evidence behind the pathway — without overclaiming.  
**Key messages:** Measured improvement; audit-led development; why training + auditing + mentoring works; pathway to excellence.  
**Content roles:** Rationale (theory + practice + feedback), role of auditing, role of mentoring, “why this works” as argument, not guarantee.  
**CTA:** Follicle Intelligence, Surgeon Development Pathway, Apply / Enquire.

---

### Apply / Enquire
**Purpose:** Single conversion page — application or serious enquiry.  
**Key messages:** Clear next step; who should apply; what to expect (e.g. review, conversation).  
**Content roles:** Who the program is for (brief), what happens after apply/enquire, form or clear contact method, optional short FAQ.  
**CTA:** Submit application / send enquiry; secondary: Training Pathways, Contact.

---

## Navigation Notes

- **Primary nav:** Home, About, Training Pathways (with dropdown: Practical FUE, Hair Loss Science, Follicle Intelligence, Surgeon Development), Standards, For Clinics, Faculty, Outcomes, Apply.
- **Training Pathways** can be both a hub page and a dropdown parent to avoid clutter.
- **Apply / Enquire** should be visually prominent (e.g. button in header).
- Structure supports future expansion: new pathway modules, new pages under Training or Standards, without redesign.
