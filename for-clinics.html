For Clinics

Structured surgeon development for clinics

IIOHR supports clinics that want to develop hair restoration surgeons through a clear pathway: practical FUE training, advanced theory, ongoing mentoring, and audit-backed improvement — not a one-off short course.

Why clinics choose IIOHR

Developing surgeons in-house or bringing new talent up to standard requires more than a single certification event.

Clinics face a common challenge: how to train or upskill surgeons in a way that is measurable, aligned with standards, and supported over time. IIOHR offers a pathway that includes hands-on FUE training, live-patient exposure, advanced trichology and hair-loss science, and post-training support through mentoring and Follicle Intelligence auditing. The result is structured surgeon development — so your team improves with clear feedback and progression, not assumption.

What we offer clinics

A pathway built for both individual surgeons and clinic-wide development.

Training for your surgeons

Practical FUE, live-patient experience, and advanced theory — the same pathway we offer individual doctors, with the option to coordinate for multiple surgeons.

Audit and standards

Follicle Intelligence and structured auditing help you see how your surgeons are progressing and where support is needed — so quality is measurable.

Ongoing support

Mentoring and development support after training so improvement continues. Beyond the short course: long-term partnership in surgeon development.

Partnership and enquiry

Whether you want to train one surgeon or build a development programme, we welcome clinic enquiries.

We can discuss how the IIOHR pathway fits your clinic’s goals — training new entrants, upskilling existing surgeons, or integrating auditing and standards into your quality framework.
