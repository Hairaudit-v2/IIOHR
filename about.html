About IIOHR

The International Institute of Hair Restoration

A premium training and standards body advancing hair restoration surgeons beyond basic short-course education — with practical FUE training, advanced theory, ongoing support, and audit-backed development.

Mission and purpose

IIOHR exists to raise the standard of hair restoration surgeon development worldwide.

We are not a generic academy or a short-format certification provider. We are an institute: we combine hands-on practical FUE training, live-patient surgical exposure, advanced hair-loss theory and trichology, and structured post-training support. Our pathway is informed by Australian surgical standards and discipline, and we work with Follicle Intelligence to support audit-backed improvement so that progression is measurable — not assumed.

Our goal is surgeon development, not just certification. We support doctors and clinics who are committed to a pathway from beginner or intermediate practice toward higher competence, with mentoring and auditing as part of the journey.

What we stand for

  • Practical and theoretical integration — Technique grounded in science; theory that informs every stage of the pathway.
  • Support after training — Mentoring and auditing continue beyond course completion so development is sustained.
  • Audit-backed improvement — Measurable progression through structured review and feedback, not assumption.
  • Australian standards — Surgical discipline and rigour as a guiding framework, with global relevance.
  • Structured progression — A clear pathway from learning to advancement, not a one-off event.

Faculty and mentorship

Development is guided by experienced surgeons who mentor, review, and support progression through the pathway.

IIOHR faculty bring surgical experience and a commitment to structured development. Mentorship runs through hands-on training, case review, and post-training support — so you are not left to close the gap between training and practice alone. That relationship is central to the institute’s standard of care.

Origin and reach

IIOHR grew from the Australian Institute of Hair Restoration and now operates as the International Institute of Hair Restoration.

Australian surgery standards and discipline remain at the heart of our curriculum and expectations. Our programmes are designed for doctors and clinics globally — whether you are based in Australia or elsewhere, the pathway is built for serious surgeon development and long-term support.
